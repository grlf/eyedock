To whom it may concern,

We are in the process of updating our lens listings for at www.eyedock.com. If you are not familiar with us, EyeDock is an online contact lens database for eye care professionals. We have steadily grown since 2003 and currently get over 1,000 visits each day.

We have included below a listing of the lenses that are currently in our database. We want to be sure we are representing your lenses as accurately as possible to best serve you and our visitors.

We would greatly appreciate it if you could look over our list of your contact information and lenses and let us know if:

  • We have your correct contact information.
  • Any of these lenses are no longer being manufactured.
  • You manufacture lenses that are not included in this list (if so, could you share the details with us?).
  • Are there any errors?
  • Is any information missing (if so, please enlighten us!).
  • Are you the primary manufacturer of these lenses, or are you a distributor?

If for some reason you cannot read the information in this email, please let us know and we will send it in another form.

We appreciate your helping us keep your lenses up-to-date in our database! Please let us know if you have any questions.

Sincerely,
Todd M Zarwell OD FAAO
